LECTURES

Combining modern technology and interesting topics for the best experience.

Beside the practical work, divided in groups, all participants will have lectures together. Lectures are held in Visnjan City Hall and are open to the public. The topics cover various areas of astronomy, and here is the tentative list of lecturers:

  • Mario Jurić, University of Washington, USA
  • Vibor Jelić, Ruđer Bosković Institute, Croatia
  • Korado Korlević, Višnjan Observatory, Croatia
  • Tomislav Jurkić, University of Rijeka, Croatia
  • more coming soon...
